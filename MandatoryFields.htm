Required Fields in the Job Details Editor

Before a job can be created or saved, the following fields must be completed in the Job Details editor:

  • Form of contract
  • Job title
  • Job reference
  • Employer
  • Contractor

Once the first draft form has been inserted, the following fields will be locked:

  • Form of contract
  • Contract Revision
  • Advance payment field

Please Note: these fields can be unlocked by deleting the draft form.

Before issuing a contract administration form, the following fields should ideally be completed:

  • Contract sum
  • Advance payment
  • Full retention
  • Retention post practical completion (Half retention)

Once a form has been issued on a job, the following fields will be locked permanently in the Job Details editor:

  • Form of contract
  • Job reference
  • Currency
  • Employer
  • Contractor
  • Contract sum
  • Advanced payment
  • Full retention
  • Retention post practical completion (Half retention)
