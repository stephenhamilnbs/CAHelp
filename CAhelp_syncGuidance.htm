Synchronising the Guidance

As the user accesses the forms in the form editor, the form guidance notes in the guidance viewer will update, ensuring that they always have the correct note for reference.
However, if the user follows the jumps to cross-referenced notes, or retrace their steps through the guidance history, the forms editor and guidance viewer will no longer be displaying concurrent information.

To synchronise the guidance viewer for the currently active form in the form editor, click the Synchronise button on the NBS Contract Administrator Guidance toolbar .
