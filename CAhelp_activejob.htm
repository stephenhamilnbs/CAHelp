Opening an Active Job

Ensure the user is currently in the Jobs view by selecting View > Jobs. In the Job list, either:

  1. Double click on a job.
  2. Right click on a job and select Edit.
  3. Select a job and click on File > Open <Job Title> from the menu.

All of the forms included in the selected job will now be displayed in the form list as shown in the example below:

From this area it is possible to carry out numerous tasks related to the forms within the active job. The user can;

  1. Add new forms to the job.
  2. Open forms for editing.
  3. Print/print preview forms within the job.
  4. Issue forms.
  5. Delete forms from the job.
  6. Recall the last issued form. Please Note: only the single most recent form issued in any job is recallable.
