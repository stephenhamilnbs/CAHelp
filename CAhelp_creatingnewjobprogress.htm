Creating a New Job in Progress

The Job in Progress in NBS Contract Administrator allows a user to incorporate details from "existing" Jobs into the software. These Jobs may already have issued contract administration forms, with associated financial implications and consequently additional information needs to be entered.

Some scenarios that may require a 'Job in Progress' include:

  • Continuing a job that was started on paper forms.
  • Re-issue a form that has been issued but cannot be recalled.
  • Amend any of the job details that have changed since the job started, but are locked in the 'Job Details' editor.

    • Before starting a Job in Progress, please ensure that all of the information from the previous Job is accurate.

    To start a new Job in Progress:

    1. Select File > New Job in progress from the menu. Alternatively click on Create new job in progress.
    2. Complete the relevant information in the Job Details, the Team tab and any relevant Sectional Completion details for this new Job.
    3. Once the mandatory fields have been filled, click on the Job in Progress... button.
    4. Within the Job in Progress Wizard, there are 3 tabs:
      • Current Status - Enter the financial information about the previous Job.
      • Sectional Completion - Please Note: This tab will only appear if details of sections have previously been entered in the Sectional Completion editor.
      • Form Numbering - Set the start number for each contract administration form number sequence.
      Fill in the relevant fields in each tab and click OK to save the changes. Clicking on Cancel will close the window.
    5. The contract administration forms can now be added to this job.
