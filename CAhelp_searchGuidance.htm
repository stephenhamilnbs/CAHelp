Searching the guidance

To open the Guidance window, either create a new job or select a Job in the Job list and click on the search icon on the Guidance toolbar.

There are 2 tabs within the Help window:

  1. Content: this tab displays all the guidance files within their relevant groups and can be expanded by clicking on the small + button.
  2. Search: here the user can manually search for a keyword or phrase then click on List Topics to display the relevant information, narrowing down their search.

If the Contents and Search tabs aren’t already visible, click on the show icon .

A Topic is opened by either double clicking on it or selecting it and clicking the Display button.
