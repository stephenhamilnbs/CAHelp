Adding Forms to a Job

Once the mandatory fields within Job Details have been completed, it is possible to add relevant forms to the active job from the appropriate Form library.

Once the first form is added to a job, certain fields in Job Details (such as the form of contract, retention etc.) are locked and will no longer be possible to edit them.

There are various ways of adding forms to the job:

  1. Drag and drop the desired form from the Form library into the Job panel above.
  2. Drag and drop the desired form from the Form library into the Forms list view in the editor.
  3. Right click on a form in the Form library and select Add to <Job Title>.

  4. On the Job menu, point to Add Form, and then click the appropriate <Form Title>.

  5. Select the 'New Form' icon from the Forms list toolbar and select the form in that drop down.

  6. Select the appropriate form and then click on the 'Add a new <Contract> form' text.

All forms that are added to an active job will be displayed in the Forms list view in the editor (this will open when the user double-clicks on any desired job).

Forms in the job have three status icons next to them:

  1. Draft: Newly added forms or forms that have not yet been issued will be displayed with an Orange Square icon and can be edited.
  2. Issued: These forms will be displayed with a Grey Circle icon and will only open in Read-Only format - they can not be edited again.
  3. Recallable: Forms that show a White Triangle icon are still recallable however this will only be applicable to the single most recent form issued in any job.
