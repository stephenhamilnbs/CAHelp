Features

Simplicity and Speed
NBS Contract Administrator has been designed to be simple and intuitive to use. Forms and Contacts are organised in the References View on the left hand side of the screen. The Editor in the centre of the screen is used for editing forms, contacts and project details. The Guidance panel on the right hand side of the screen displays contract administration and editorial guidance, which is synchronised with the content of the Editor. Users of other NBS products will find the use of NBS Contract Administrator familiar, as it has been designed to complement existing NBS products.

Extensive Guidance
To support users at the point of decision making, extensive guidance is provided in the software. The guidance provides detailed advice and information about contract administration and communication and will be regularly updated to pick up changes arising from case law and in legislation. The guidance panel on the right of the software is synchronised with the content of the Editor. This provides quick and easy way of editing forms.

Automation and Accuracy
NBS Contract Administrator supports rapid and accurate communication by automating many aspects of completing the forms. For example, company names and addresses are added automatically and values for retention, VAT and payment are calculated from the valuation entered for interim and final payments. NBS Contract Administrator manages project contacts and distribution lists.

Audit Trail, Archive and Printing
Detailed records of contract communication are essential. NBS Contract Administrator maintains comprehensive records, storing all forms as PDF copies for record purposes The quality of the audit trail is maintained because once a form has been issued it will not be editable. All forms can be accurately printed from NBS Contract Administrator. Printing provides a true preview with support for Windows printers.

Content
NBS Contract Administrator supports various forms of contract:

Standard Contracts
  • JCT Standard Building Contract 2005 (SBC05)
  • JCT Standard Building Contract 2011 (SBC11)
  • JCT Standard Building Contract 2016 (SBC16)
  • SBCC Scottish Standard Building Contract 2005 (SBC/SCOT05)
  • SBCC Scottish Standard Building Contract 2011 (SBC/SCOT11)
Design and Build Contracts
  • JCT Design and Build Contract 2005 (DB05)
  • JCT Design and Build Contract 2011 (DB11)
  • JCT Design and Build Contract 2016 (DB16)
Intermediate Contracts
  • Intermediate Building Contract 2005 (IC05))
  • Intermediate Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2005 (ICD05)
  • Intermediate Building Contract 2011 (IC11)
  • Intermediate Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2011 (ICD11)
  • Intermediate Building Contract 2016 (IC16)
  • Intermediate Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2016 (ICD16)
Minor Works Contracts
  • Minor Works Building Contract 2005 (MW05)
  • Minor Works Building Contract with Contractor's Design (MWD05)
  • Minor Works Building Contract 2011 (MW11)
  • Minor Works Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2011 (MWD11)
  • Minor Works Building Contract 2016 (MW16)
  • Minor Works Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2016 (MWD16)
  • SBCC Scottish Minor Works Building Contract 2005 (MW/SCOT05)
  • SBCC Scottish Minor Works Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2005 (MWD/SCOT05)
  • SBCC Scottish Minor Works Building Contract 2011 (MW/SCOT11)
  • SBCC Scottish Minor Works Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2011 (MWD/SCOT11)
  • SBCC Scottish Minor Works Building Contract 2016 (MW/SCOT16)
  • SBCC Scottish Minor Works Building Contract with Contractor's Design 2016 (MWD/SCOT16)
Other Contracts
  • Generic forms. for possible use with contracts not listed above.

At the heart of the system are the well-known RIBA forms for contract administration. NBS Contract Administrator is the only software available that is licensed to use these RIBA contract administration forms.
