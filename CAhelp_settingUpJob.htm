Setting up a New Job

NBS Contract Administrator stores information on jobs in order to produce accurate financial reports and to ensure that consistent job descriptions and addresses are printed on contract forms. Below are descriptions of all the fields involved in creating a new job.

Please accurately complete all the job fields before adding any contract administration forms.

Job Details:

  • Job title - A short title for the job.
  • Form of contract - Identifies the form of contract being used on the job, which effects the selection and completion of the contract administrator forms.
  • Job reference - A unique short reference to identify the job, such as the job number, file reference or other identifier within the office. This reference is also used to construct the file names of of issued forms generated as PDF documents automatically. For example, the 2nd Instruction to be issued on job reference IBA_04_20 will be named IBA_04_20_Instruction_2.pdf and saved in the selected output location.
  • Currency - Select the form of currency to use throughout the job. It is possible to use $ (Dollars), Ł (Pound) or € (Euro).
  • Form logo - This option will be populated by default with the details previously set up in the Tools > Options > Form logo dialog. Changes made to the form logo option in the job details editor will apply to the active job only. The selected form logo will appear in the bottom left hand corner of any printed contract administration form.
  • Output location - A location on the local or network drive to store the exported output of jobs.  This is where all exported forms and reports in PDF (Portable Document Format) or Excel format will be stored for a particular job.

    • Works:

  • Description - A short description of the job.
  • Telephone - Telephone contact number for the job.
  • Fax - Fax number for the job.
  • E-mail - Email contact for the job.
  • Address - Contact address for the job.
  • Post Code - Contact post code for the job.

    • Contract Conditions:

  • Employer - Select from the list of companies which are identified as Employers. If the company does not appear in the drop down list, it should be created using the Address Book.
  • Contractor - Select from the list of companies which are identified as Contractors. If the company does not appear in the drop down list, it should be created using the Address Book.
  • Contract Sum - Enter the contract sum. Please Note: do not alter this contract sum as adjustments are made within the forms. Calculations will be done automatically and any amendments will be displayed in the Current Status panel.
  • Advance Payment - Enter any Advance Payments made. Please Note: This field is not available on minor works forms of contract.
  • Contract Dated - The date the contract was signed.
  • Commission Date - The date the commission was received.
  • Base Date - Enter the base date of the job as stated in the contract.
  • Date of Possession - The date of possession by the contractor of the site as stated in the contract.
  • Original Completion Date - The date stated in the contract as the date for the work to reach practical completion. Please Note: The Original Completion date should not be adjusted as the job progresses. Revised completion dates are amended automatically and displayed in the Current Status panel.
  • Rectification Period - Enter the defects liability period in days, months, weeks or years.
  • Rate of Damages - Enter the rate of damages (in days, months, weeks or years) which may be levied against the contractor in the event of late completion.
  • Time Period Allowed for Final Payment of Payment Certificates – Enter the time period allowed (between 1 and 31 days) for the final payment of payment certificates. This value is used to auto calculate the Final date for payment field on Interim Certificate and Directions, Certificate for Interim Payments and Certificate of Progress Payments.
  • Full Retention - Up to Practical Completion a percentage of the job value will be retained. Edit the default value if necessary.
  • Retention Post Practical Completion - After Practical Completion, half of the retained percentage will be kept while the rest is released. Edit the default value if necessary.

    • The Current Status section of the job details editor will automatically update when any relevant contract administration forms that are issued.
