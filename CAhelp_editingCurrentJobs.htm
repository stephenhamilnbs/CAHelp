Editing Current Jobs

There are 3 ways to edit an existing job:

  1. Double click on the job in the Jobs list.
  2. Select the job and go to File, Edit <Job Title>.
  3. Right-click on the job and select Edit.

The first two options will open the list of forms for that job, whereas the last option will open the Job Details tab.

Please note: some fields in the Job Details tab will be locked after the first form is inserted.
