Payment figures are calculated automatically and we normally round figures to the nearest £1 - how do I override this?

The Amount Payable field on a payment certificate is automatically populated from the retention amount (taken from Job Details), but users can manually adjust this to enter their own figures.

In the Payment Certificate, click on the button for the Amount Payable field and the following Edit Amount window will appear:

Select the option to Enter amount manually and enter the desired amount in the box. Click on the OK button to save changes and close or the Cancel button to exit.
