Sending Feedback

As with all NBS subscription services, NBS provides software support via the NBS Customer Centre on 0345 456 9594.

NBS values all feedback and encourage users to contact us with information they wish to share that we can use to further develop NBS Contract Administrator.
The user can select Help on the Menu bar and choose Send Feedback... to send an email to the support group support@thenbs.com.
