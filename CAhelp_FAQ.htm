Frequently Asked Questions

It is important to us that the users feel confident in using NBS Contract Administrator. To help overcome any problems they may have, we have listed some of the questions commonly asked of Software Support, along with the appropriate answers:

  1. Which fields in the Job Details Editor are mandatory to fill in?
  2. How do I re-order my Jobs list?
  3. How do I hide cost information on an Architect/Contract Administrator’s Instruction?
  4. All of my certificates have the Serial Number prefixed to SNUM - how can I change this?
  5. What are Items/Instruction Types and how do I use them?
  6. I've finished a contract and would like to remove it from the list. How do I do this?
  7. How can I add custom Roles to my job and include them in the Transmittal Sheet?
  8. Why can't I change my Issued Form back to Draft to correct an error?
  9. Payment figures are calculated automatically and we normally round figures to the nearest £1 - how do I override this?
  10. What is the Draft Number on an Instruction and why does it print on the Issued Form?
  11. Is there a way to spell check my forms before issuing them?

The user can visit the NBS Contract Administrator Support Page and have access to more articles and videos on using the software.

Alternatively they can contact the Software Support team on 0345 456 9594 Monday to Friday 9am to 5pm where they will be happy to help.
